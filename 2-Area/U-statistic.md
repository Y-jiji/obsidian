# Definition

U-statistics is a family of functions: 
$$
f: {n:NN} -> RR^(d times n) -> RR
$$
such that
$$
exists {r: NN}::forall n >= r:: f_(n)(x_1,dots, x_n) = frac((n-r)!,n!) sum_(sigma: {1,2,dots,r} -> {1,2,dots,d}\ sigma "is injection") f_r (x_(sigma(1)), dots, x_(sigma(r)))
$$
